Cargo Services

Our cargo services are designed to meet your logistics needs with efficiency and reliability. Whether you’re transporting urgent goods, perishable items, or bulky cargo, we provide tailored solutions to ensure your shipments are delivered safely and on time. With a fleet that includes the ATR-72F for regional operations and the Boeing 737-300F for larger loads, we are equipped to handle both short and long-haul flights. Our key destinations include Mombasa, Kisumu, and Nairobi, as well as regional hubs like Entebbe, Dar es Salaam, and Addis Ababa.
We also serve other major cities across East and Central Africa, offering connections to destinations like Juba, Kigali, and Lubumbashi. Whether your cargo requires regional distribution or international shipping, our experienced team and advanced fleet ensure that your goods are handled with care and precision every step of the way. Let us help you connect your business to key markets with dependable cargo solutions tailored to your needs.
